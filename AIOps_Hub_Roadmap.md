# AIOps Hub -- Consultant Journey

## Mission

Build a production-ready AI Automation platform that serves as: - A
flagship portfolio project - A reusable consulting platform - A
foundation for a premium AI consultancy

## Target

-   Land \$2,000--10,000 AI automation projects
-   Reach \$3k--5k/month from international clients
